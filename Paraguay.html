Learn more about Paraguay

Paraguay, officially the Republic of Paraguay (in Guaraní: Paraguái Tavakuairetã), is a landlocked American country located in the area central South America. It is a bilingual country. Language predominant native, the Guaraní, has coexisted for centuries with the Spanish and is the source of an immense poetic production (oral and written), expressed in musical and literary compositions valued throughout the world world. Likewise, it is the language of everyday life, spoken by the majority of the inhabitants.
